ABOUT ME

My Path

I was formely the Lead
Designer at Mixpanel, where I
had the opportunity to build
one of the strongest brand in
Analytics. In January, I will join
forces with Digital Asset, a
young company who wants to
change the future of Finance.

Over the past ten years, I also joined
forces with clients of all sizes, from
all around the world, and worked as
one to re-brand their marketing site
or bring their ideas to life. Together
we collaborated in an iterative
process of design and ongoing
optimization.
